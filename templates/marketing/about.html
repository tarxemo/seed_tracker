{% trans "About the Programme" %}

{% trans "Digitizing Seed Distribution in Mbeya" %}

{% blocktrans %}Mbeya Seed Tracker exists to make government seed distribution transparent, accountable and fast — from the moment seed stock arrives in the region to the moment a farmer confirms it in their own hands.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Our Purpose" %}

{% blocktrans %}Smallholder farmers depend on receiving the right seed, in the right quantity, at the right time. When distribution is tracked on paper across five organizational levels, records get lost, quantities get disputed, and farmers are left waiting without answers. This system replaces that paper trail with a single, auditable record that every level of the chain can see - scoped strictly to their own territory.{% endblocktrans %}

  • {% trans "Actual confirmed collection by the farmer - not just a paper signature" %}
  • {% trans "A full activity log for accountability at every level" %}
